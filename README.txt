
Hello Miners,
I am so glad to announce that we are finally finished converting our Mod to 1.18, this is especially exiting because the caves naturally generating in this version are perfect for a DRG based mod. But we didn't just update it to the new version, we also added a couple new things, like the Seasoned Moonrider, the Glyphid slammer, and a few more.

In this mod, we imported ores/minerals like Morkite or Nitra from the awesome game "Deep Rock Galactic" into Minecraft.
 
The highlight of this mod is that you can brew beers in a brewery (Lloyd) which have special effects just like in DRG. The recipes are the same as in drg. Also, Lootbugs will now spawn within the world.
 
The ores (Nitra, Morkite, Red sugar), hops for brewing also generate in the world, just like Ommoran Heartstones.
 
Lastly, we have our four supported Languages:
LOLCAT
English (US)
English (upside down)
last and very much least, German
 

with regards,

TheTrueSCP and MonsieurHannes

Rock and Stone, Lads!
